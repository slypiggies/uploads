## Controls
Mouse: Camera

Arrow keys: Movement

`X`: Toggle normal mapping

`Y`: Toggle Earth rotation

`W`: Increase brightness of lighting 2 (violet color)

`S`: Decrease brightness of lighting 2 (violet color)
